AIVAH, a community of IT, AIML, and CSIT students, unites innovators and learners to create impactful tech solutions. Focused on collaboration, education, and social responsibility, AIVAH hosts hackathons and workshops on AI, Machine Learning, and Data Science. Members gain practical skills, foster creativity, and work as teams, with support from mentors and industry resources. At AIVAH Community, we’re not just about events and projects; we’re about building a legacy of innovation, collaboration, and impact. Our members are empowered to explore, experiment, and excel, equipped with the skills, network, and resources to shape a future driven by technology


ZENITH

Zenith, an exclusive influencers' meet organized by the AIVAH Community, brings together visionary creators, thought leaders, and innovators under one roof. This dynamic event is a platform to exchange transformative ideas, share impactful stories, and spark meaningful collaborations that drive progress. With a focus on innovation, influence, and inspiration, Zenith celebrates the power of community and creativity in shaping a better future. Join us in this extraordinary gathering as we connect, collaborate, and rise to new heights together.










DEVIGO

DEVIGO— Develop, Innovate, Go—is a community dedicated to empowering creators and thinkers to turn ideas into reality. We focus on nurturing skills, inspiring innovation, and driving forward momentum in every project. DEVIGO brings together passionate individuals to collaborate on meaningful initiatives, pushing boundaries and creating solutions that positively impact our communities and industries.
